1 · Pair your Vector (tab above) — needs wire-pod running once.
2 · Put Vector on a table with ~1 m of free space, robot connected (green).
3 · Open the lens on Spectacles, set WS_URL to this Mac's IP.
4 · Calibrate the surface with your hand, place Vector on the glowing mark, press START.
