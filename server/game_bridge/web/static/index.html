How to play

1 · Pair your Vector (tab above) — the Bluetooth path sets it up, no typing.
2 · Put Vector on a table with ~1 m of free space, robot connected (green).
3 · In Lens Studio set GameConfig.WS_URL to the address shown in the "Lens WS_URL" card above, then send the lens to Spectacles.
4 · Calibrate the surface with your hand, place Vector on the glowing mark, press START.
